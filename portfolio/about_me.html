Get to know a little bit about Me

I've been an artist all my life and a gamer since i got my first NES at 5 years old, i am constantly evolving from drawing, sculpting and painting, to being a folklore dancer (did it for 15 years) to modeling, doing professional make up, body painting live canvasses to also being a live canvass myself, to playing drums to writing poems, i worked as an actress as well (music videos and as an extra on commercials and movies) i am a cosplayer i create costumes, i also make props and outfits for carnivals and events, and now i am studying and working to become a software engineer.
